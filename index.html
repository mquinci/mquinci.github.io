About Me

Committed to problem-solving and research

I am a curious person who is exited by the opportunity to learn new information and skills. I developed a love for learning through my experience homeschooling. This form of schooling gave me the opportunity to learn in unconventional ways including countless museums visits, historical role-play, and unique projects. After taking my first research methods class in college, I became enamored by the research process. I was excited by the idea that conducting an experiment could help answer a question that no one in the world has the answer to. All aspects of the research process fascinated me: from designing an experiment to control for certain variables, to collecting and organizing the data, and interpreting the results.
